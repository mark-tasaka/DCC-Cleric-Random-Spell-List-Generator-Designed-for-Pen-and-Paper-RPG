Dungeon Crawl Classics Cleric Spell List Generator

Welcome to the Launch Page of the Dungeon Crawl Classics Cleric Spell List Generator. The Cleric Spell List Generator is designed to generate a listing of Cleric Spell appropriate to the spell level selected. For each spell a random spell manifestation is selected. A page number, which makes reference to the page that the spell is found in the DCC rulebook, is listed beside each spell, and the spells are listed numeric order.
